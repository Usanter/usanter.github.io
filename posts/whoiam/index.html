Biography:

I did my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer science: Artifical Intelligence and pattern recognition at the University of Toulouse 3 (France). Currently, I’m a Ph.D. student in Inesc-ID in Lisbon (Portugal), under the supervision of Pr. Alberto Abad. My research focuses on developing speech therapy games for children with speech disorder. Mainly by studying the automatic processing of children pathological speech. I do this PhD in the context of Training Network on Automatic Processing of PAthological Speech (TAPAS)

During Masters’s internship, I worked on Sparse Coding/Dictionary learning versus Deep neural network to extract new features in a signal. During this year, I also worked on satellite image recognition using Deep learning methods.
